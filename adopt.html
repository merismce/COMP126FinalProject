Adopt/Foster

At Paws & Purpose, we are dedicated to finding loving homes for our cats. Whether you're looking to adopt a new furry friend or provide temporary care through fostering, we're here to help!

Adoption Process

To adopt a cat, please visit our Meet the Cats page to see our available felines. Once you've found a cat you'd like to adopt, fill out our adoption application form and schedule a meet-and-greet.

Fostering Opportunities

Fostering is a wonderful way to help cats in need while providing them with a safe and loving environment. If you're interested in becoming a foster parent, please contact us at pawsandpurpose@gmail.com.
